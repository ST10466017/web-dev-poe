About BrightFutures

BrightFutures is a community organisation based in Cape Town focused on reducing food insecurity and improving educational outcomes for children. Our mission is to empower children with nourishment and knowledge, creating opportunities for long-term success.

History

Founded in 2019, BrightFutures started as a small volunteer-led food drive and has grown into several feeding schemes and after-school programmes across Cape Town.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide nutritious meals and educational support to underprivileged children.

Vision: A South Africa where no child goes to bed hungry and every young person has the opportunity to succeed.

Leadership

Our team comprises local community leaders, educators, and volunteers committed to transparent and impactful work.

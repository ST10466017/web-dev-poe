About BrightFutures

BrightFutures is a community organisation based in Cape Town focused on reducing food insecurity and improving educational outcomes for children.

History

Founded in 2019, we began as a volunteer-run food drive and have grown into multiple feeding and learning programmes.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide nutritious meals and educational support to underprivileged children.

Vision: A South Africa where no child goes to bed hungry and every young person has the opportunity to succeed.

Leadership

Our team is made up of educators, community organisers, and volunteers committed to impact and transparency.

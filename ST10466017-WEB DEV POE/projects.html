Our Projects

Community Feeding Schemes

We run community kitchens that prepare nutritious meals distributed to children and families in need. The programme operates weekdays at three partner locations.

After-school Tutoring

Volunteer tutors provide literacy and numeracy support twice a week to primary school learners to boost classroom performance.

Mentorship and Life Skills

Workshops on life skills, hygiene, and career awareness for older learners and youths in the community.
